about=The CC Monitoring Toolbox provides a simple GIS framework for climate-resilient urban monitoring and planning, developed as part of the EU-funded Interreg North Sea Project. It is aimed at decision-makers across the North Sea region. The cooling module addresses thermal comfort through the analysis of physiologically equivalent temperature (PET) to identify heat-stress hotspots, high-resolution shadow mapping, and the evaluation of surface material properties. For urban greening, the toolbox measures the quantity and quality of vegetation. The water management component focuses on infiltration potential, providing essential data to support the transition toward blue-green infrastructure concepts and mitigate local flood risks. By integrating these features into a single modular platform, the CC Monitoring Toolbox helps planners effectively assess the success of their cooling projects.
